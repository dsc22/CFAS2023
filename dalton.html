About Dalton

Dalton Clifford

Financials Since | 2013

Dalton brings over seven years of accounts payable, receivables, and assistant controller experience in a variety of industries. He has dual degrees in Accounting & Finance from the University of Alabama and is currently expanding his skills in Graphic Design and Web Development (including programming this website). In the wake of the COVID-19 Pandemic, Dalton decided to break from his career as an Account Executive and return to the family business.

Dalton leverages a creative side to tackel unique financial positions and challenges, specializing in software integrations and data dashboards that add layers of visibility into a business' financials. In addition to financial accolades, Dalton has three years of experience consulting and advising Fortune 2000 brands on their online market research methodology and packaging design. He brings with him unique insights from 100+ research initiatives medium and large sized business' leveraged for their financial strategy and decisions, including go-to-market and pricing strategy.

In his free time Dalton enjoy long walks with his dog Maverick, Music Festivals, Art, and History. He's an avid traveler and life long learner.
