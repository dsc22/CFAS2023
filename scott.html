About Scott

Scott Clifford

Financials Since | 1987

As it's founder, Scott established CFAS in 2008 to assist small to mid-market companies that have the need, but limited budgets for an executive level financial partner. He works with business' from $3 to $53 million in yearly revenue in industires such as, but not limited to, medical, retail, distribution, manufacturing, and professional services.

Scott has dual degrees in Accounting & Business Administration and brings over 30 years of experience in financial strategy, building cash flows, raising capital, and leading multi-member accounting teams to increased levels of accuracy and efficiency. He takes a unique approach to his clients everyday challenges and believes through a combination of careful cost, risk management, and strengthening cash position any business can and will prosper.

Born and raised in the bluegrass state of KY, Scott initially moved south to pursue a baseball career before resolving to move to Cincinnati with his wife Natalie. There they raised their two boys and have since settled north of the city. Scott spends his free time with family and enjoying the atmosphere's of his Cincinnati Reds and SEC baseball.
